IA: Identified Anomaly

Green: Safe, entity poses no danger.

Orange: Hazard, entity capable of causing harm if provoked.

Red: Dangerous, entity is hostile.

Purple: Apollyon, entity is hostile and is capable of causing a containmemt breach.

Gray: Unknown/Undecided, entitys class has not been decided or does not have a class.
